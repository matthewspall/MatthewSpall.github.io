Project Title 1

The purpose of the recommendation report is to improve the understanding of voter outreach and voter contact through canvassing, and to highlight areas of improvement to better communicate and market to voters. The research and proposals were delivered to the management of the campaign on December 1st, 2024. The research was conducted through the collection of qualitative data as taking notes of conversations with contacted voters when canvassing. The canvassing was done as part of the Patricia Nilsson for Ada County Highway District Commissioner campaign, characterized as a low information municipal election.
